About Us

R & R Financial Planners Inc

Founded in 1979, R&R Financial Planners, Inc. is an SEC Registered Investment Advisor with offices in New York and Florida. As a registered advisor, we adhere to the Investor Advisors Act of 1940, providing comprehensive services that meet the highest standards. Led by William V. Romeo, C.F.P.Â® and Nancy Praino, our team brings decades of experience in financial planning, helping clients across the country build secure financial futures.

At R&R Financial Planners, we simplify the complexity of financial planning. Our experts work closely with you to develop a personalized plan that suits your unique needs. We ask the right questions, evaluate the best strategies, and explain everything in clear terms. By continuously monitoring and managing your plan, we ensure you stay on track to reach your goals. This commitment to excellence and client-focused service sets us apart from other financial planning firms.
